## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a company">
    Paste a careers page URL. JobBeacon detects the job board automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your filters">
    Add keywords (like `senior backend` or `product designer`) and locations (like `Remote` or `Berlin`). Optional but recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We keep watch">
    JobBeacon checks each company regularly. Existing jobs are seeded silently — only *new* postings count as new.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You get an email">
    When something matches, we send a single email with the new roles and a link to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>
