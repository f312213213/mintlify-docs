---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Quick answers to the questions we hear most."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How often does JobBeacon check for new jobs?" icon="clock">
    Every company is polled **once per hour**. So at worst, you'll hear about a new role about an hour after it's posted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why didn't I get an email about a job I saw on the company site?" icon="circle-question">
    Three usual suspects:

    1. **Filters didn't match.** Open the company in JobBeacon and check the matched count. Try loosening keywords or locations.
    2. **The job was already there when you added the company.** First-scan jobs are marked as *seed* and don't trigger emails — only roles posted *after* you started watching.
    3. **Notifications are muted** (globally or for that company). Check **Settings**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does Workday show 'N Locations' instead of real cities?" icon="briefcase">
    Workday's job listing API doesn't return per-location strings for multi-location postings. JobBeacon shows "N Locations" in the table and lazy-loads the real list when you hover the cell. For filter matching, turn on **Include unspecified locations**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add a company that doesn't use Greenhouse / Ashby / Lever / etc.?" icon="building">
    JobBeacon needs a structured job board to read from. If a company runs a fully custom careers page with no underlying ATS, JobBeacon can't watch it. See [Supported platforms](/guides/supported-platforms) for the full list.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get notifications somewhere other than email?" icon="bell">
    Email only, today. Slack and webhook delivery are on the roadmap — let us know if you'd find them useful.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there native mobile apps?" icon="mobile">
    No — JobBeacon is a web app. It works on mobile browsers, and emails are designed to be readable on a phone.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I share a job with someone who doesn't have an account?" icon="share-nodes">
    Yes. Click the share icon on any job row and send the link. The recipient gets a public job preview without needing to sign up. See [Sharing jobs](/guides/sharing).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will JobBeacon apply to jobs for me?" icon="paper-plane">
    No. JobBeacon notifies you about new postings. The application is on you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I delete my account?" icon="trash">
    Everything tied to your account — watched companies, filters, job history, share links — is deleted. See [Account & billing](/guides/account-billing#deleting-your-account).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a status page?" icon="signal-bars">
    Yes — [status.jobbeacon.app](https://status.jobbeacon.app). It shows live uptime for the API and the hourly polling job.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I report a bug or request a feature?" icon="bug">
    Email [support@jobbeacon.app](mailto:support@jobbeacon.app). Include the URL or company name involved if it's about detection or filtering.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
