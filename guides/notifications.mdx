JobBeacon sends notifications by email. There's no app to install and no browser permission to grant — if you can receive mail, you can receive alerts.

## How alerts work

- Every company you watch is checked on a regular schedule.
- For each company, new jobs are matched against your filters.
- If anything matches, you get **one email per company** listing all the new matching roles.
- If nothing matches, no email — JobBeacon is quiet by design.

The email comes from `notifications@jobbeacon.app`. Add it to your contacts to keep it out of spam.

## Two levels of control

JobBeacon has two switches that work together.

### Global toggle (Settings → Notifications)

Your master switch. When **off**, JobBeacon stops sending you any email — even if individual companies have notifications on. Use this when you're on vacation or just want a break.

### Per-company toggle (company page → Filters card, or Settings)

Each company you watch has its own switch. Mute one without affecting the others.

### How they interact

| Global   | Company  | Result               |
| -------- | -------- | -------------------- |
| On       | On       | You get emails       |
| On       | Off      | Muted for that company only |
| Off      | On       | Muted (global wins)  |
| Off      | Off      | Muted                |

<Tip>
  If your global toggle is off and you turn a single company **on**, JobBeacon automatically flips the global toggle on too. We assume turning a company on means you want to start hearing from JobBeacon again.
</Tip>

## Pausing without losing data

Muting a company (global or per-company) only stops the **email**. JobBeacon keeps polling and tracking new jobs in the background. When you turn notifications back on, you'll still see the full history on the company page — you just won't get a backfill email for jobs posted while muted.

## I'm not getting any emails

Quick checklist:

1. **Global toggle** in Settings is on?
2. **Per-company toggle** is on for the company you expect to hear from?
3. **Filters** aren't too narrow? Open the company page and check the "matched" count.
4. **Inbox / spam.** Search your mail for `notifications@jobbeacon.app`.
5. **Account email.** Make sure the email address Clerk has on file is one you actually check. You can change it from your profile.

If none of that helps, [email support](mailto:support@jobbeacon.app).

## I'm getting too many emails

A few options:

- **Tighten filters.** Add more specific keywords or locations. See [Filters](/guides/filters).
- **Mute noisy companies.** Use the per-company toggle.
- **Drop companies you no longer care about.** Open the company page and click **Remove**.
