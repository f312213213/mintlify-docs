## Your account

JobBeacon uses [Clerk](https://clerk.com) for accounts. From your profile (avatar → **Manage account**) you can:

- Change your email address
- Change your password
- Add or remove sign-in methods (email, Google)
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Delete your account

Notification emails always go to the **primary email** on your account. If you change it, the next alert lands in the new inbox.

## Theme

JobBeacon has light and dark modes. Toggle from the avatar menu — your choice is saved to your account, so it follows you across devices.

## Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your avatar → **Settings** → **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a plan">
    Click **Upgrade** next to the plan you want. JobBeacon redirects you to Stripe Checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment">
    Stripe handles payment. You're redirected back to JobBeacon when done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    Your new limits apply immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your subscription

From **Settings → Billing**, click **Manage subscription**. This opens Stripe's customer portal where you can:

- Update your payment method
- Download invoices
- Switch plans
- Cancel

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you keep Pro features until then.

## Deleting your account

From your profile (avatar → **Manage account**), scroll to the bottom and click **Delete account**. This removes:

- Your watched companies
- Your filters and notification preferences
- Your job history
- Your share links

If you have an active Pro subscription, cancel it first from **Settings → Billing**.
