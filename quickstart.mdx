---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Set up your first job alert in under 5 minutes."
---

By the end of this guide you'll be watching at least one company and have notifications turned on. No setup, no install — everything happens in the browser.

## 1. Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Open [jobbeacon.app](https://jobbeacon.app) and click **Get started**. You can sign up with email or Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    JobBeacon sends notifications to the email on your account, so make sure you can receive mail there.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The free plan is enough to try things out. You can upgrade later from **Settings → Billing**.
</Tip>

## 2. Add your first company

You only need a URL — JobBeacon figures out which job board the company uses.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    From the dashboard, click **Add company**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste a careers URL">
    Anything works:

    - The company's careers page (`https://vercel.com/careers`)
    - A direct job board URL (`https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/acmecorp`, `https://jobs.lever.co/binance`, etc.)

    Click **Add**. JobBeacon detects the platform and starts an initial scan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the seed scan">
    The first scan loads every job currently on the board. You'll see live progress. These existing jobs are marked as *seed* — you won't get emailed about them. Only roles posted *after* this scan count as new.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Don't see your company show up? See [Supported platforms](/guides/supported-platforms) for what works and what doesn't, and what to do when detection fails.
</Note>

## 3. Set up filters

Filters are optional, but without them you'll get notified about *every* new job at that company. For most users, that's a lot.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the company page">
    Click the company you just added.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add keywords">
    Add words that should appear in the job title. Examples:

    - `backend`, `infrastructure`, `platform`
    - `product designer`, `design system`
    - `senior`, `staff`, `principal`

    Choose **Match any** (an OR — match if *any* keyword appears) or **Match all** (an AND — every keyword must appear).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add locations">
    Add locations to filter by. Examples: `Remote`, `Berlin`, `New York`, `Taiwan`. Same any/all toggle as keywords.

    Some boards (like Workday) don't always include real location strings. Toggle **Include unspecified locations** if you want those jobs to come through anyway.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Filters are forgiving — `full-stack`, `full stack`, and `fullstack` all match each other. Capitalization doesn't matter.
</Tip>

## 4. Confirm notifications are on

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your avatar → **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the global toggle">
    The **Email notifications** switch at the top is your master kill-switch. Make sure it's on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the per-company switches">
    Below the global toggle you'll see every company you watch with its own switch. New companies are on by default.
  </Step>
</Steps>

See [Notifications](/guides/notifications) for how the toggles interact and how to mute things temporarily.

## 5. Wait for the next scan

JobBeacon polls every company **once an hour**. When a new role matches your filters, you'll get an email like this:

> **New roles at Acme Corp**
> – Senior Backend Engineer · Remote
> – Staff Platform Engineer · Berlin
>
> [View 2 new roles at Acme Corp →]

That's it. You're done.
